Course

A course can be a mix of presentations, case reviews, instrumentation, treatment therapies, surgical techniques and endovascular techniques with the addition of a hands-on lab. Courses are typically led by three to ten HCP Consultants with experience to meet your education needs. HCPs are able to share knowledge with peers and faculty. Courses may be located at one of our locations or at another training facility.

Symposium

A panel of experts who discuss their experiences on a particular topic before a group of HCPs, to include: faculty, fellows and residents. The symposium is meant to be interactive with the panel answering attendees’ questions on solving tomorrow’s challenges today.

Peer-to-Peer

These programs are designed to provide an opportunity for experienced HCPs to discuss current trends with their colleagues. Participants contribute through case-based presentations assigned by the course chairs; the objective is to enhance the HCPs’ knowledge and skills in treatment options for their patients. This interactive program is designed for a small number of participants.

Tutorial

A tutorial accommodates a small group of faculty and participants. The program typically includes observation of live surgery, didactic sessions and a group dinner. A second half-day session may include hands-on anatomic training or bioskills opportunity and case discussion.

Platform for Education and Knowledge (PEAK)

PEAK Events allow the HCPs to engage in lively discussion and debate with other HCP experts through an informal setting close to home. THe HCP faculty provides lectures and/or case reviews on current clinical issues relating to the effective use of DePuy Synthes products. Faculty could be present at the dinner or participating via real-time video conferencing.

Worldwide Customer Visit (WWCV)

A HCP or surgical team from the same institution visits a DePuy Synthes site for a day. There is a customized agenda to meet the visitor’s needs. The agenda typically includes product discussion and an introduction to DePuy Synthes employees. This may also have a lab component if requested.

Small, Market Appropriate, inteRactive, Training (SMART) Lab

These technique intensive programs are focused on the practicing HCP. SMART Labs may be particularly effective for experienced surgeons using a competitive system who have an interest in changing their operative protocol to DePuy Synthes products or for experienced health care workers who provide the day-to-day patient care.

OR Visitation

Designed to provide the opportunity to meet experienced surgeons in either a one-on-one setting or in a small group. Participants observe live surgery and discuss surgical technique, indications and clinical results. This may also have a lab if requested and available.

Raynham, MA

Our flagship location, The DePuy Synthes Institute in Raynham, Massachusetts is the largest education facility of its kind of the East Coast. The Institute houses a sub-dividable auditorium, with seating for 200; an additional six classrooms with seating for 25–60; and multiple laboratory spaces to accommodate up to 19 stations for hands-on-training. State-of-the art audiovisual and communications equipment allow broadcasting to remote locations and from room to room within the facility. You are invited to explore the versatility of the DePuy Synthes Institute, and advance your expertise in this exceptional learning environment.

Palm Beach Gardens, FL

The DePuy Synthes Institute in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida is located at our DePuy Synthes Power Tools headquarters. This facility houses a 2,700 square foot wet lab which is equipped with 21 microscopes and can accommodate up to 8 c-arms. A sub-dividable classroom can accommodate up to 80 participants via classroom seating or 100 theater style. The DePuy Synthes Institute is proud to continue the Anspach legacy of training surgeons and operating room personnel in Palm Beach Gardens.

West Chester, PA

The DePuy Synthes Institute in West Chester, PA is located at our DePuy Synthes headquarters for our Biomaterials, CMF and Trauma businesses. This location offers a 2,765 square foot lab accommodating up to 4 c-arms. Lecture space and on-site catering can be reserved in conjunction with hands-on programs.

Norderstedt, Germany

For over 20 years, the European Surgical Institute (ESI) has stood at the forefront of equipping today’s surgeons with the skills of tomorrow, teaching pioneering surgical techniques to more than 10,000 healthcare professionals each year from a variety of medical backgrounds. We operate in a fast-moving environment with some of the brightest minds in medicine, and critical to our longevity has been the ability to continually adapt our offering to meet the changingneeds of the surgeons and patients we serve.

Sharjah, United Arab Emirites

The Sharjah Surgical institute (SSI) is a state-of-the-art, surgical training facility that revolutionizes healthcare professional training and development in the Middle East. SSI is the outcome of a pioneering partnership that links the University of Sharjah’s College of Medicine with Johnson and Johnson Middle East and Olympus Medical. The SSI comprises a superb operating theater, dry labs, cadaveric lab and virtual reality simulation lab.
