    Description

    A Worldwide Customer Visit is an educational-based program designed to provide Healthcare Professionals with an opportunity to experience the Warsaw campus and meet with the DePuy Synthes Joint Reconstruction professionals who bring passion and commitment to our products.     • Interact with DePuy Synthes professionals in Marketing, R&D, and Manufacturing
    • Increase knowledge of specific DePuy Synthes Knee products
    • Gain hands-on product and procedural experience through a cadaveric lab
