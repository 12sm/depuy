Veterinary Education

DePuy Synthes Vet has been serving veterinary surgeons since 1969. In 2005, DePuy Synthes launched DePuy Synthes Vet, it's new division dedicated to animal orthopedics.

This division has a dedicated product development team and in-house sales consultants providing technical support to veterinary surgeons. DePuy Synthes Vet attends more than a dozen regional and national meetings each year in the U.S. DePuy Synthes is the only manufacturer of original equipment of the AO Foundation.
